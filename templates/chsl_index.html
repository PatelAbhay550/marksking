About SSC CHSL Examination

The Staff Selection Commission Combined Higher Secondary Level (CHSL) examination is conducted for recruitment to posts like Lower Division Clerk (LDC), Junior Secretariat Assistant (JSA), Postal Assistant (PA), Sorting Assistant (SA), and Data Entry Operator (DEO). Our calculator supports Tier-I Computer Based Examination with subjects including General Intelligence, Quantitative Aptitude, English Language, and General Awareness.
